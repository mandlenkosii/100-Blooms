Our Story

Rooted in
Passion & Blooming Dreams

100 Bloom was born from a love of flowers and a desire to bring beauty into everyday life. Founded in 2020 by a team of passionate florists, we started as a small local shop in Johannesburg and have blossomed into a beloved floral studio known for our handcrafted bouquets and personalized service.

Our Values

At 100 Bloom, we are committed to excellence in every aspect of our work. Our core values guide us in creating beautiful floral arrangements that bring joy and meaning to every occasion.
